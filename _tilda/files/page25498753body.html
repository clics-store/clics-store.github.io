We would like to propose the following format of collaboration:

From our side:

increased author compensation
90% of the actual price sold goes to the author (standard author's royalty 80%)
Personal online support
Clics developers and marketing team are ready to help you.
Promotion through our brand instagram of your work with a link to your showcase
We share your work 3 times a month on our CLICS Instagram (feed and stories).
Verified CLICS profile
Your CLICS profile will be verified with a checkmark.
Unique bonuses for author-partners
Further seasonal collaborations and promotions, lower withdrawal limit.
From the author's side:
Put a link in your Instagram BIO to your personal CLICS profile (clics.store/your_nickname)
This link leads only to your images. Use this for at least a month.
Publish announcements of images you want to sell and attach a direct link to these images on your Instagram stories
At least 3 stories and 2 posts a week with a link to your showcase (generated automatically). We recommend to publish announcement regularly.
Tag our CLICS instagram profile once a week
Tag @clics.store at least once a week in your post or stories (as you prefer).
After the end of the collaboration period, you will have an increased author compensation (90% instead of 80% for all users).

If you are interesed, please sign in to CLICS and we will contact you soon.
