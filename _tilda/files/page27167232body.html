Personal license
The licensed asset can appear in non-commercial projects.

Can be used for:
  • End Products Not For Sale
  • One personal social media account with non-commercial activities
Cannot be used for:
  • End products for sale
  • Business social media accounts
  • Physical or digital paid advertisements
  • Native apps, web apps, or games
Commercial license
The licensed asset can appear in up to 5,000 end products for sale.

Can be used for:
  • Up to 5,000 physical or digital end products for sale
  • One business social media account owned and managed by the licensee
  • Unlimited physical advertisements for local markets
  • Digital paid advertisements with unlimited impressions
  • Broadcast and streaming for up to 500,000 lifetime viewers

Cannot be used for:
  • Native apps, web apps, or games
Exclusive license
The licensed asset can appear in up to 250,000 end products for sale.

Can be used for:
  • Up to 250,000 physical or digital end products for sale
  • Unlimited business social media accounts owned and managed by the licensee
  • Unlimited physical advertisements for local, national, and global markets
  • Digital paid advertisements with unlimited impressions
  • Broadcast and streaming for unlimited lifetime viewers
  • One native app, web app, or game downloaded or sold up to 250,000 times

FAQ

In this section you will find answers to the most popular questions.
How to purchase a photo?

Choose a photo you like and click the "Buy" button.

Enter your bank card data in Stripe and confirm the transaction.

You can download the original image and the license immediately after the successful purchase.

What am I actually buying?

You are buying the rights to use the image in your personal or business purposes.

Should I buy a subscription?
No registration or subscription needed to use CLICS. You can make one-time purchases and come back whenever you like.
How do I get the photo and license after purchase?
After the successful payment, you will be redirected to a file and license download page. All files will be duplicated to the email address you provided during payment.
Will there be a payment check?
A copy of the receipt will be sent to the email address you entered in transaction details.
How can I use this photo?
You can use photos under the terms of the purchased license.
Read the license terms and conditions here https://clics.store/licenses
Do I need to make any prior arrangements with the author?
No. All images posted on the author's personal page are available for purchase and further use under the terms of the purchased license.
Can I buy exclusive rights?
Сurrently, we offer three types of Royalty Free licenses: Personal, Standard and Extended.
If you need an Exclusive license, please get in touch with legal@clics.store
I believe the photo violates my copyright. How do I file a complaint?
Please email us at legal@clics.store.
Include "Copyright" in the subject line.
In the message, please include:
  • A link to the image.
  • A detailed description of why you believe it is in violation of copyright.
  • Your information and contact details
