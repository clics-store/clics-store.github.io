Privacy Policy of CLICS
10 october, 2022

This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) sets out how Clics Store, Inc (“CLICS”, “we” or “us”) and (or), if applicable, its controlled subsidiaries and affiliates, uses and protects your personal data (“Data”) that you provide to us for the registration on the website https://clics.store/ (“Website”) or for downloading and using our mobile application ( hereinafter referred to as “Mobile application”),and uses the cookie files and other automatic data collection tools in connection with your use of the Website . The Website and the Mobile application together are referred to as the “Clics Store Services”.

We kindly ask you to read this Policy carefully. If you have any questions, please contact us on legal@clics.store.

By using the Clics Store Services , you acknowledge and give your consent to the collection, use, processing and transfer of your Data by CLICS in accordance with this Policy.
CLICS takes the obligation to protect your privacy. This Policy explains how we collect, use, process and share your Data, and how we use cookies. It also describes the choices available to you for using your Data and how to access and update this Data. The terms of this Policy apply to all Data collected by CLICS on or through the Clics Store Services .
This Policy applies to anyone who visits and (or) signs up/authorizes the Website or downloads and uses our Mobile application.

1. Types of Data processed by CLICS
a. Clics Store Services authorization Data.
So, we are clear about the terminology we are using, when we use the “Data” in this Policy, we mean information that is capable of being associated with or could reasonably be linked with you, such as your mobile number or email address, used for the registration your account in Facebook, and your public profile in Facebook (including your name/nickname and profile photo).

Thus, to use the Clics Store Services you have to:
- authorize via Facebook entering the email/phone number and password used for the registration in Facebook, or
- authorize via Google entering name, the email/phone number and password used for the registration in Google; or
- authorize via Apple entering the email/phone number and password used for the registration in Apple.

Passwords are collected and stored encrypted using an automated process.
We do not need and do not ask for the access to any other Data from your Facebook, Apple or Google account.

b. If you become a member of CLICS content, when you register as a member or change your CLICS account details, certain additional information, we may request and process background information about you and your content that we believe is necessary to assist us in marketing your content, as well as identifiers that allow us to verify your identity or your rights to the content you provide to us.
In each case of the request of such information we will ask for your consent for processing such Data, if there are no any other legal grounds for processing of such Data.

c. In addition to the foregoing, for each visit to the Website, CLICS collects the IP address and type of device accessing the website (such as a mobile phone or desktop computer, information about the operating system of your mobile device, about your browser, browser language or operating system; about your wireless network, etc.) to analyze usage and trends and personalize and improve the Website . We store and associate this Data with your account on the Website.

d. When you download and use our Mobile application, we automatically collect information on the type of device you use, and operating system version. We do not ask for, access or track any location-based information from your mobile device at any time while downloading or using the Mobile Application.

e. When you send feedback via email or provide us with an error report, we will obtain your username, email address, and email content, so that we can communicate with you about problems encountered during the use of the Clics Store Services.

2. Processing of Data
We use the Data that we have about you to operate, provide, support and make our Clics Store Services more relevant and useful to you and others.
We may process Data by the ways such as collection, recording, organization, structuring, storage, retrieval, consultation, use, disclosure by transmission, dissemination or otherwise making available, alignment or combination, restriction, erasure or destruction (hereinafter collectively referred to as “Processing”).
We process your Data on the ground that such processing is necessary to further our legitimate interests, including: (1) providing effective and innovative Clics Store Services to our users; and (2) to detect, prevent or otherwise address fraud or security issues in respect of our Clics Store Services, unless those interests are overridden by your interest or fundamental rights and freedoms that require protections of Data.

Also, we may use the Data we collect from and about you to:
-Authorize you on the Clics Store Services or create your user account;
-Provide you with information about your account;
-Administer your account;
-Investigate, prevent or take action regarding illegal activities and/or violations of our Terms of Service;
-Send information including confirmations, invoices, technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support and administrative messages;
-Respond to customer service requests, questions and concerns;
-Improve our service;
-Keep you informed about special offers and services of CLICS, as well as CLICS marketing initiatives;
-Сomply with laws and regulations.

3. Sharing of Data
We may share your Data we collect from and about you (1) for legal, protection, and safety purposes; (2) to comply with laws and in response to lawful requests and legal processes; (3) to protect the rights and property of CLICS; and (4) with those who need it to do work for us (our Third Party service providers, as defined above, as well as our auditors and services provides), (5) with any other third party with your prior consent to do so.

We will not sell, rent, trade or otherwise transfer any Data outside of CLICS and, if applicable, its controlled subsidiaries and affiliates, without your explicit permission, unless it is obliged to do so under applicable laws or by order of the competent authorities.
We may disclose Data to respond to legal requirements, exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims, to protect our interests, fight against fraud and to enforce our policies or to protect anyone’s rights, property, or safety.

4. Storing of Data.
Unless otherwise specified, we retain Data for as long as it is necessary and relevant for us to achieve the above goals or to ensure compliance with our legal obligations to protect Data. After terminating your user account on any of the Clics Store Services we will store the minimum amount of data about you that we are required to store in accordance with the law or other legal requirements. We may store activity data on the condition of anonymity in order to improve our Clics Store Services.
Data collected by CLICS may be stored and processed in USA, or any other country in which CLICS or, if applicable, its affiliates or agents have facilities.
We reserve the right to transfer your Data outside of your country. By using the Clics Store Services, you consent to any such transfer of information outside of your country. In any case, we will ensure the adequate level of protection of your Data in such a country.

If CLICS or substantially all of our assets are acquired by a third party, Data held by us about our users will be one of the transferred assets. Such third party will be required to treat that Data in a manner consistent with this Policy.

5. Сookie
We may use cookies and other tracking technologies to make it easier for the Website users to use the Website, for example, to track information about activity on the Internet or other electronic network, such as information about Website users' preferences, Website activity and the IP address of devices that users of the Website use to access the Website, which may include identifiers and geolocation information. The information allows us to associate such information with the CLICS user accounts of the Website and provides verification of their personally identifiable information. Cookies and other tracking technologies are also used to collect general statistical information about usage and volume, which does not include personally identifiable information.

Disabling / enabling cookies through your browser
There are several ways to manage cookie and other tracking technologies. In your browser settings, you can accept or reject cookies, or set your browser to ask you before accepting cookies from the websites you visit. You should be aware that you may not be able to use all of our interactive features if you set your browser to complete disabling of cookies.
If you use different computers in different places, you need to make sure that each browser is configured according to your preferences.
You can delete any cookies that have been set in your browser's cookie folder. Different browsers provide different procedures for managing your preferences. For instructions, click any of the browser links below.
Microsoft Windows Explorer
Google Chrome
Mozilla Firefox
Apple Safari
If you do not use any of the above browsers, you should select "cookies" in the "Help" function for information on where to find your cookie folder. You may not be able to selectively opt out of all third party advertising cookies placed on our website. You should also be aware that disabling cookie does not disable other types of analytics products that we may use to collect general information about how you and other visitors use our Website.
If you are located in the European Union, you can renew or change your consent to the use of cookie.

At the same time, we require third parties to deal with shared information with confidentiality and security measures no less than those required by this policy.
