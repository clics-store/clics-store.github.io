Frequently Asked Questions

In this section you will find answers to the most popular questions.
How do I purchase a photo?

Choose a photo and click on the "buy" button.

Enter your bank card data in Stripe and confirm the transaction.

You will be able to download the image and the license immediately after the successful purchase.

Am I buying the photo or the rights to use the photo?
You are buying the rights to use the image (image file included).
Can I buy one photo or do I have to buy a subscription?
There is no subscription at the moment. You can buy each photo in a couple of clicks without registration.
How do I get the photo after I purchase it?
When the successful payment is made, you will be redirected to a file and license download page.
How do I get the license file for the photo?
You will be redirected to the download page and the license file.
All the files will be duplicated to the email address you provided during payment.
Show more
How can I use this photo?
You can use these photos under the terms of the purchased license.
You can read the license terms and conditions here https://clics.store/licenses
How do I get a payment check?
A copy of the receipt will be sent to the email address you entered in your transaction details.
Do I need to make prior arrangements with the author to buy and the terms of purchase?
You don't need to do that. All the images that are posted on the author's personal page are available for purchase and further use under the terms of the purchased license.
I want to buy an exclusive photo, how do I do that?
You can contact the author and ask him to give you an exclusive license in this case.
I believe the photo violates my copyright, how do I file a complaint?
Please email us at legal@clics.store.
Include "Copyright" in the subject line.
In the message, please include:
  • A link to the image.
  • A detailed description of why you believe it is in violation of copyright.
  • Your information and contact details
